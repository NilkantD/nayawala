Hyper-Deflationary​

Tifffin's circulating supply decreases every minute of every day. Over 1% of every transaction is immediately and automatically burned forever, and as the number of burned tokens increases, the burn rate accelerates.​

Passive Income​

A portion of every Tifffin transaction is immediately and automatically reflected back to all Tifffin holders. Yes, this means you earn more Tfn just by holding Tfn in your wallet. Passive income for everyone.​

Governance Token​

Being that Tifffin is the governance token of Tifffin, you can stake it there to earn multiple extra streams of passive income. In addition, Tifffin transactions buy and burn TifffinToken, accelerating the reduction of supply.​
